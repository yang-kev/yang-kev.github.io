About:

  • I am a fifth-year PhD student in mathematics at Stanford University. My advisor is Amir Dembo. Before that, I studied mathematics at Harvard University.
  • I am current a Program Associate at MSRI for "Universality and Integrability in Random Matrix Theory and Interacting Particle Systems".
  • My research interests are in probability and large-scale behaviors, for example connections between interacting particle systems, PDEs, and stochastic PDEs.
