About:

  • I am a Benjamin Peirce fellow at Harvard University. I was an NSF postdoc at the University of California, Berkeley and Harvard University.
  • My research is at the intersection of probability and analysis (e.g. KPZ, stochastic analysis, random matrices, and N-body problems more generally).
  • I organize the Harvard Probability Seminar.
  • I am also a (trail) runner. See the end of this webpage for more details.
